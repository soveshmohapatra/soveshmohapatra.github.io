TinyGPT from Scratch

A 150-line decoder-only Transformer in pure PyTorch — multi-head causal self-attention, pre-norm residual blocks, weight tying, the GPT-2 recipe. Trained on synthetic 3-digit addition (with the output-reversal trick) to 100% accuracy in 98 seconds, with a per-position learning curriculum that exposes the carry-chain bottleneck.

Optimizers Head-to-Head

Five optimizers from scratch (SGD, Momentum, Adam, AdamW, Lion) — 75 lines of PyTorch total. Benchmarked on Rosenbrock, Beale, and a real MLP from identical initialisation. Momentum beats Adam by six orders of magnitude on smooth valleys; Adam beats everything on flat plateaus; on real training they all converge to within 0.2 percentage points.

Development Status Completed

BPE Tokenizer from Scratch

Byte-level byte-pair encoding implemented in 80 lines of pure Python. Compression curves at vocab sizes 300–2000, the linguistic structure BPE discovers from frequency alone, and the failure mode where 116 characters of repetitive code collapse into a single token — the silent foundation behind every LLM behaviour that depends on tokenisation.

Development Status Completed

Live Project May 2026

Normalization Layers Head-to-Head

BatchNorm, LayerNorm, RMSNorm, and GroupNorm implemented from scratch and benchmarked head-to-head on a 20-layer MLP from identical initialisation. RMSNorm beats LayerNorm on every measured axis — fewer parameters, faster training, half the FLOPs — confirming the design choice every modern LLM (Llama, Mistral, Gemma) made.
